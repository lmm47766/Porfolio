A Little About Myself

I am and have always been a problem solver. This kind of methodical thinking landed me a degree in Statistics and in Applied Mathematics. In my early career I applied my skills and abilities as an analyst. A combination of patience and knowledge quickly bumped me from my former title to Supervisor. Once I knew the ins and outs of marketing analytics, I decided to peruse my interest in web development where I am now, attending the Coding bootcamp at UCLA. My ultimate goal is to become a full stack developer.
