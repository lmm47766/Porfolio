About Me

A Little About Myself

I am and have always been a problem solver. This kind of methodical thinking landed me a degree in Statistics and in Applied Mathematics. In my early career I applied my skills and abilities as an analyst. A combination of patience and knowledge quickly bumped me from my former title to Supervisor. Once I knew the ins and outs of marketing analytics, I decided to peruse my interest in web development where I am now, attending the Coding bootcamp at UCLA. The bootcamp focused around the MERN (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js) stack. I have experience utilizing HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, Bootstrap, Firebase, React.JS, Node JS, MySQL, Mongo DB, Express, Handlebars JS, Ruby, Python, ES6, Amazon Web Services, Git & Materialize.
